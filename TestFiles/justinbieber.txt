Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and global pop icon. He rose to fame in 2009 after
 being discovered on YouTube, where he uploaded videos of himself singing. His debut single "One Time" and first 
 album "My World" made him an instant teen sensation. Bieber's music blends pop, R&B, and dance, earning him a massive 
 fanbase known as "Beliebers." Over the years, he has released multiple chart-topping albums like "Purpose," "Justice," 
 and "Believe," featuring hit songs such as "Sorry," "Peaches," "Baby," and "Love Yourself."
